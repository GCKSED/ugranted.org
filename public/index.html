We help new and existing businesses succeed in Grant County, Kansas.

UGranted's (Grant County, Kansas Economic Development) comprehensive business services and programs provides companies, back-office support, research, manufacturing, or distribution sectors with everything they need to expand in the Greater Southwest Kansas Region.

Grant County welcomes everyone to its community. In the southwestern Kansas region, Grant County is known for its family-friendly atmosphere and stunning views of the prairie landscape. The 24-square-mile area has a population of 7,077 residents who enjoy the many recreational opportunities within this thriving community.

  • Population of 7,077
  • Access in the Southwest Kansas Region gives access to potential employees across the tri-state area in Kansas, Oklahoma, and Colorado.
  • Industries include agriculture, dairy, livestock, oil, natural gas, and renewable wind.

Featured Business

Pioneer Communications has been providing telecommunication services to Southwest Kansas since 1957. They have expanded to bring reliable, affordable high-speed internet and phone services to homes and businesses. Pioneer is the best internet provider in Southwest Kansas, providing the highest quality customer service in the region. Year after year, they show their commitment to customer service and are the best choice for communication services in the Southwest Kansas region.

Invest in Grant County

The Economic Development Committee works to catalyze proactive economic development and drive land development and business development projects. The committee has identified three growth areas for the county: growing human capital, business capacity, and prosperity for residents and businesses in Grant County and the Southwestern Kansas Region.
